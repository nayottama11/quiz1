Jakarta's Cuisine

SOTO BETAWI

Soto Betawi is a creamy and aromatic beef soup hailing from Jakarta. It's made with tender beef, coconut milk, and a fragrant blend of spices, resulting in a rich and savory broth that's often served with rice, fried shallots, and lime wedges.

KERAK TELOR

Kerak Telor is a delightful Jakarta street food specialty. This crispy rice omelette is cooked with egg, shrimp, and various seasonings. It's a perfect balance of textures and flavors, offering a satisfying crunch with a hint of seafood essence.

NASI UDUK

Nasi Uduk is a beloved Indonesian dish featuring fragrant coconut rice. It's typically served with a variety of side dishes, such as fried chicken, sambal (spicy chili paste), fried shallots, and various condiments.

KETOPRAK

Ketoprak is a delightful noodle dish commonly found in Jakarta. It consists of rice noodles, bean sprouts, tofu, and rice cakes, all drenched in a delicious peanut sauce. It's often garnished with emping crackers and fried shallots.

SOTO TANGKAR

Soto Tangkar is a hearty beef rib soup originating from Jakarta. It features tender beef ribs simmered in a flavorful broth made with herbs and spices. The dish is commonly enjoyed with rice and a side of emping crackers.

SEMUR JENGKOL

Semur Jengkol is a unique Indonesian dish made with jengkol beans, which have a distinctive aroma. These beans are stewed in a sweet and savory sauce, often accompanied by ingredients like tofu or tempeh.
